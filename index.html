ABOUT

My favorite cartoon is shinchan . Crayon shinchan is a Japanese cartoon. It is written and illustrated by Yoshito Usui. This cartoon was first started in 1990. This cartoon is one of the very popular cartoon of India. This cartoon is not only loved in India but is also very liked in other foreign countries like Netherlands, Belgium, Italy, Germany, Spain etc. This cartoon is about the adventures of a five year old boy shinchan. He was a very naughty but sweet child. There are many adventures in his life. In his family their is mother(Mitsy), father (Hiroshi) and a sister (Himawari). He has many friends too. His friends name are kasama, Suzuki, masav , nany and I Chan. There is a story behind it's making. The news is viral that shinchan was a real boy of Japan. his mother asked him to go for shopping. while shopping be didn't found Himawari there and then began to search her. he saw that Himawari was going towards road. so in order to save her he ran towards her but unfortunately he and Himawari both became the prey of the accident and died on the spot. Their mother started to feel alone and to remove her loneliness she started a comic strip which was bought by a company and they named the cartoon as crayon shinchan./
